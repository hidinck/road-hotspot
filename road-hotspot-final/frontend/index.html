🛣️ Smart City Infrastructure · Jalandhar, Punjab
Road Accident Hotspot
Discovery System
An AI-powered platform that uses unsupervised machine learning (DBSCAN) to automatically detect dangerous road accident zones in Jalandhar, Punjab — from raw GPS trajectories to actionable safety insights in real-time.
01
📡
GPS Data Collection
Vehicle GPS coordinates collected at 10Hz. Includes latitude, longitude, speed, IMU data. In production, connect to iRAD or live IoT vehicle sensors.
02
⚗️
Feature Engineering
Raw GPS enriched with: jerk, danger proximity score, event classification, time-of-day, road type, rolling statistics per vehicle window.
03
🧬
DBSCAN Clustering
Density-based spatial clustering with Haversine metric. Groups nearby accident-prone points. ε=95m, min_samples=5. Noise labeled −1.
04
⚖️
Risk Scoring
Each cluster scored 0–100: density×40% + severity×35% + frequency×25%. Labels: Low/Medium/High/Critical based on score bands.
05
🚨
Real-Time Alerts
14 vehicles traverse Jalandhar every 3 seconds. Haversine check against all cluster radii. Zone entry triggers immediate severity-classified alert.
06
📊
Analytics & Insights
Interactive Leaflet map, Chart.js analytics, AI-generated textual insights, model comparison (DBSCAN vs K-Means) — all live.
